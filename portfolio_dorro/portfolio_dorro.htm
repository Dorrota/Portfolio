Loki and Valdi

Loki is the Black Standard Poodle. Before I met him I hadn't realise poodles are so cool - intelligent and even-tempered. But the dog I loved the most was Valdi, a small mongrel with a big temperament.

Safari and zoo.

During my French holidays I visited the African reserve. My family (my husband and two daughters) was impressed. We didn't have to go to Africa to experience a real safari. The goats in the picture were to play with children. And a donkey is a symbol of French Catalonia.

Butterflies everywhere

The outdoor butterfly aviary is just one of many beautiful places to visit in Languedoc-Roussillon, the region in France close to Spanish border. The big blue butterflies were the most impressive but hard to catch them in a camera.
